Mini encyclopedia

The goal of this website is to gather detailed information about different aspects of cooking, from concepts and techniques to ingredients.
The information is meant to be backed by science, easily understandable and accessible. In the future, readers will have the possibility to provide feedback to help fact check the articles.


Stance on AI

The contents of this website were researched, written and redacted without the use of LLMs.
Generative AI was and will not be used for this website.


Note about information related to health

The information relayed on this website about the influence of some foods or techniques on health should not be taken as medical recommendations.
Please always consult a health professional regarding health-related decisions, a few hours of research can never replace years of studying and practicing.
